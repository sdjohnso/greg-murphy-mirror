Understanding the Votes

Vote Types

Passage
Final vote on whether a bill becomes law
Amendment
Vote on a proposed change to a bill
Motion to Recommit
Send bill back to committee (often a minority party tactic)
Rule
Sets the terms for debate on a bill
Concurrence
Agreeing with changes made by the other chamber

Floor Procedures

Suspension
Fast-track procedure for non-controversial bills. Limited debate, no amendments, requires 2/3 majority to pass.
Under Rule
Standard procedure with full debate and amendments allowed. Simple majority (1/2) required to pass.
